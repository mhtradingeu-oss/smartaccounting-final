# Core Features

## User & Company Management
- Multi-company support
- Role-based access (Admin, Accountant, Auditor, User)
- Company profiles and tax settings

## Accounting
- Invoices lifecycle
- Expenses management
- Bank statements & reconciliation
- VAT logic (UStG aware)

## Reporting
- Dashboards with KPIs
- PDF & CSV exports
- Audit trails

## Notifications
- Email alerts
- Slack-ready
- Real-time feed (WebSocket planned)
