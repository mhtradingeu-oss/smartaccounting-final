# Product Vision & Scope

## Vision
To become the most trusted AI-powered accounting platform in Germany by embedding compliance, intelligence, and automation directly into daily financial operations.

## Target Users
- SMEs and Startups
- Freelancers and Consultants
- Accounting firms (Steuerberater)
- Auditors and financial controllers

## Core Principles
- Compliance-first (German law before automation)
- Explainable AI (no black-box decisions)
- Auditability and transparency
- Role-based intelligence
- Multi-language accessibility
