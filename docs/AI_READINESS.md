## German Tax Advisory Scope

### AI Limitations
- AI does NOT submit tax declarations
- AI does NOT generate official DATEV or ELSTER files
- AI does NOT automate VAT or tax classification
- AI does NOT perform any tax submission or filing

### AI Advisory Only
- AI can explain VAT gaps, detect inconsistencies, prepare exports, simulate readiness, and answer "why" questions
- All AI outputs are insights and explanations only
- No action, submission, or automation is performed

### Mandatory Disclaimer
- “SmartAccounting does not submit tax declarations.”
